Swimming

I started swimming at a young age and have continued it to date. From the age of 5 until I was 11 I just did lessons but from the age of 12 and upwards I began training competitively and competed in competitions all over Ireland with my local swimming club Kingdom Swimming. From the start of 3rd year until Christmas of 6th year I was training 6 times a week, 5 days a week. After school on Monday,Wednesday and Friday and in the morning on Wednesday and Sunday. Training would usually start at 5:15pm and finish at 7:00pm.

Life Saving

I have complete all the training to become a qualified lifeguard who can work on all beaches in Ireland. It took me 3 years to complete as i could only do it during the summer months. I am also qualified to preform CPR on a patient.
